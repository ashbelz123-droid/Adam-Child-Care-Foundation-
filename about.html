About Our Foundation

Giving hope, care and opportunity to vulnerable children.

Who We Are

Adam Child Care Foundation Uganda Ltd is a non-profit organization located in Budaka District, Eastern Uganda. The foundation was created to support vulnerable children who lack access to basic needs such as education, health care, and a safe environment. Through compassion, community support, and partnerships, we work to transform the lives of children and give them a brighter future.

Our Mission

To provide care, protection, education, and opportunities for vulnerable children so they can grow into responsible and empowered individuals.

Our Vision

A community where every child is valued, protected, educated, and given the opportunity to achieve their dreams.

Our Values

Love • Compassion • Integrity • Service • Hope

Founder

Mukwege Adam founded Adam Child Care Foundation with the vision of helping vulnerable children receive love, care, and education. His passion is to ensure that every child, no matter their background, has the opportunity to grow and succeed.
