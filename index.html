About Adam Child Care Foundation

Adam Child Care Foundation is committed to improving the lives of children facing poverty, lack of education and limited access to healthcare. Through community support, partnerships and volunteer work, our foundation provides programs that help children grow, learn and thrive.

Our goal is to create a safe and nurturing environment where children are protected, empowered and inspired to reach their full potential.

Our Programs

Education Support

We help children access school materials, tuition assistance and mentorship programs that encourage learning and academic success.

Food & Nutrition

Our feeding programs ensure children receive nutritious meals and support healthy development in vulnerable communities.

Community Outreach

Through community visits and outreach programs we provide care, guidance and support for families and children in need.
