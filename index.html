Our Mission

Adam Child Care Foundation Uganda Ltd is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children through care, education, protection and empowerment. We believe every child deserves safety, love and the opportunity to dream for a better future.

Founder

Mukwege Adam

"Every child deserves love, dignity and opportunity. Our mission is to give hope and a future to children who need it most."
