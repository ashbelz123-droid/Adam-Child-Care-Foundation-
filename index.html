About Our Foundation

Adam Child Care Foundation works to improve the lives of disadvantaged children and families. Our mission is to create safe environments where children can learn, grow and thrive regardless of their background. Through partnerships, community outreach and donor support we provide essential services that transform lives.

Our Programs

Education Support

We help children access quality education by providing school materials, tuition support and mentorship.

Food & Nutrition

Our feeding programs ensure vulnerable children receive healthy meals to support growth and learning.

Community Outreach

We engage communities through awareness campaigns, health initiatives and child protection programs.
