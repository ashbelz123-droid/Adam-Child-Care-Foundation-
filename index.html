Giving Hope To Vulnerable Children

Every child deserves love, education and the chance to dream about a better future. Adam Child Care Foundation Uganda Ltd works to support vulnerable children through education, care and community support programs. Together we can restore hope and transform lives.

Adam Child Care Foundation was created to ensure that vulnerable children receive love, protection, education and the opportunity to grow into responsible and empowered adults. With the support of volunteers and partners, we believe every child can have a brighter future."
