Adam Child Care Foundation Uganda Ltd

Giving hope, love and opportunity to vulnerable children in Uganda.


Our Mission

Adam Child Care Foundation Uganda Ltd is a non-profit organization committed to transforming the lives of vulnerable children through care, education, support and hope. We believe every child deserves a safe home, quality education and a chance to dream for a better future.

Founder

Mukwege Adam

"Every child deserves love, dignity and a future filled with hope. Our mission is to protect, support and empower children who need it most."

Our Programs

Child Education

Providing school support, materials and mentorship to help children achieve their dreams.

Food Support

Ensuring children receive nutritious meals and basic needs for healthy growth.

Community Care

Working with communities to create safe environments for vulnerable children.
