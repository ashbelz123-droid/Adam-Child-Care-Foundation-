About Our Foundation

Adam Child Care Foundation Uganda Ltd is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping vulnerable children receive care, protection, education, and opportunities for a better future. Our mission is to provide love, hope, and support to children who need it most.

Message From The Founder

"When I started Adam Child Care Foundation, my dream was simple — to give every child a chance to grow, learn, and feel loved. Many children face difficult situations, but together with caring people like you, we can change their future. Every child deserves hope."

— Mukwege Adam
