Our Programs

Adam Child Care Foundation Uganda Ltd runs several community programs designed to support vulnerable children and strengthen families in Budaka District and surrounding communities.

Our Core Activities

Education Support

Providing school fees support, learning materials, mentorship and guidance to ensure children stay in school and achieve their dreams.

Feeding Program

Ensuring vulnerable children receive nutritious meals that support healthy growth and learning.

Community Outreach

Working with families and local leaders to protect children and improve community wellbeing.

Child Protection

Promoting children's rights, safety and access to basic needs such as education, shelter and health.
